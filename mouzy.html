The Mouzy Effect.
Real Play. Real Impact.

How Zuzy Run turned everyday customers into active players, driving engagement, repeat purchases, and real business growth for Mouzy.

The Challenge

Mouzy, a leading retail and dining destination, wanted to increase repeat store visits, boost sales across multiple outlets, and build a stronger digital relationship with their customers.

Traditional loyalty programs were seeing declining engagement. They needed an experience that was interactive, rewarding, and highly viral.

Zuzy Run Integration

A custom 3D endless runner game connected directly to reward payouts.

The Solution

Gamefaktory launched Zuzy Run, a high-fidelity 3D mobile runner game accessible instantly via QR code scans at physical outlets. Customers scanned their receipts or table QR codes to play.

Players collected virtual coins as the Mouzy mascot ran through obstacles, unlocking instantly redeemable rewards (coupons, free drinks, discounts) that drove foot traffic back into stores.
